📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | Power BI
📌 Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project developed to analyze employee data and identify factors influencing employee attrition. The dashboard provides HR managers with valuable insights into workforce demographics, employee satisfaction, salary distribution, experience, and department-wise attrition.

The project helps organizations make data-driven HR decisions to improve employee retention and workforce planning.

📉 Key Insights
Administration department has the highest employee count.
Employees earning 6–10 LPA show the highest attrition.
Most employees belong to the 26–35 years age group.
Male employees represent approximately 60% of the workforce.
Job satisfaction has a significant impact on employee attrition.
Employees with lower experience tend to leave more frequently.
